## Product Definitions

### Core Product Concept: "The Executive Agent"
An AI agent capable of taking high-level strategic commands (e.g., "Optimize our cloud infrastructure spend", "Audition candidates for the Senior Engineer role") and autonomously planning, executing, and verifying the work across multiple external systems.

### Key Features
1. **Autonomous Execution Engine**
   - Description: Self-correcting loop that plans, acts, observes, and refines.
   - Value: Removes the human from the loop for complex tasks.
   - Priority: High

2. **Secure Enterprise Integrations**
   - Description: Deep, secure connectors to AWS, Jira, Salesforce, Slack, etc.
   - Value: Allows the agent to do real work in the real world.
   - Priority: High

3. **Audit & Oversight Dashboard**
   - Description: Full transparency into every action taken by the agent.
   - Value: Builds trust and compliance for high-ticket clients.
   - Priority: Medium

### Target Market
- **Primary Audience**: CTOs and COOs of mid-to-large enterprises.
- **Use Cases**: DevOps automation, HR recruiting pipelines, Financial auditing.
- **Value Proposition**: "Hiring an AI employee, not buying a software tool."
